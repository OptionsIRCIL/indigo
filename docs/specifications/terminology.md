# Terminology

## Abstract

To write software that is both adherent to standards and commutable to CNSes
other than Options IRCIL, Indigo adopts most of its terminology and design
practices from [Inform USA](https://www.informusa.org/)'s
[CNS Standards v10.1](https://www.informusa.org/standards).
