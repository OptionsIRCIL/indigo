# Contributing

## Licensing

By submitting a contribution to Indigo, you agree to the [Developer Certificate of Origin 1.1](https://developercertificate.org/).

If you wish to license your work under any license other than the license(s) specified in LICENSE.md, you MUST include an SPDX license expression as part of your contribution.

For example:

```go
// SPDX-License-Identifier: BlueOak-1.0.0
```

We encourage the use of a strong copyleft license, such as [AGPL-3.0-or-later](https://www.gnu.org/licenses/agpl-3.0.html), in your contribution, along with the relevant SPDX license expression.

However, we respect your right to license your contributions more permissively if you wish.
